Brainstorm Creative Resources
Principal UX Engineer (Remote | FT Consultant | Ongoing)

OPPORTUNITY OVERVIEW:

Brainstorm Creative Resources is supporting a leading national media organization that is looking to engage a Principal UX Engineer in a 100% remote, full-time (40 hours/week), open-ended contingent role that may also offer an opportunity to transition into a full-time, salaried, staff position.

This role is open to qualified applicants living anywhere in the United States. The commitment will require ongoing availability to work M-F during east coast business hours. We do not expect any evening or weekend work to be required.

We welcome applicants who would only like to be considered for the contingent role and we also invite interest from individuals who would like to be considered for the contingent role AND are ideally seeking a full-time position.

We'll be glad to share plenty of additional information about the employer with well-qualified applicants. We’ve worked closely with the hiring manager over the last few years, to help her identify UX Designers for temporary and full-time roles.

ROLE DESCRIPTION:

The Principal UX Engineer is an integral part of the employer’s Product Design and UX team. As they partner with stakeholders across the enterprise, they frequently engage with teams to take their business goals and user needs and translate them into usable, desirable product experiences.

The Principal UX Engineer will work with cross-functional teams and Product Designers to develop front-end interfaces that engage their customers via websites, native apps and distributed platforms. They will produce high-quality customer experiences that elevate customer satisfaction, trust, and delight; while maintaining usability, accessibility, brand integrity standards.

The purpose of this role is to combine user-centered design expertise with front-end code implementation and apply those skills across multiple products and projects. This ideal candidate excels at advanced CSS3 front-end development, user interface design and translating complex information and process into intuitive interfaces.

The Principal UX Engineer demonstrates proficiency in multiple sub-specialties of UX engineering (front-end coding, CMS, Sprint planning, Agile development, etc.)  and is a thought leader on the team and to the organization at large.

•    Degree or certification in product design, visual design, UX design or related field
•    6-9 years in a UX engineer role, including at least one year in a lead role
•    Demonstrated expertise in HTML5 and JavaScript

•    Experience with leveraging AI tools to accelerate or enhance UI/UX design and frontend development products and workflows
•    Experience in collaborating with other team members to ensure optimal performance and usability
•    Ability to work effectively on a team that embraces agile principals and lean product development lifecycle
•    Understand how to translate enterprise design solutions across break points and/or key platforms (mobile, app, third-party); with a focus on responsive, mobile experiences
•    Knowledge of and practical experience in working closely with Product Management and Engineering teams; informing roadmaps, and sprint planning and product releases
•    Write efficient, maintainable, and scalable frontend code using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript

•    Ability to implement frontend enhancements, including advanced design patterns, animation, micro interactions, and data visualization in web interfaces
•    Capable of partnering with backend developers to integrate frontend interfaces with APIs and services
•    Strong communication and presentation skills (verbal and written)
•    Strong organization skills with the ability to independently prioritize and manage time effectively across multiple projects
•    Ability to offer and accept feedback constructively
•    Self-motivated, inquisitive
•    Adaptable; open to changes in organization or process
•    Desire to stay ahead of emerging trends
•    Portfolio of work that demonstrates required skills
